=============
Need Support?
=============

There are various ways to get support for the news extension!

.. _support-stackoverflow:

Stackoverflow
-------------

Please use `Stackoverflow <https://stackoverflow.com/questions/tagged/legacy-param-guard>`_
to get the best support and tag your question with `typo3` and `legacy-param-guard`.

.. _support-slack:

Slack
-----

The generic channel `#typo3-cms <https://typo3.slack.com/messages/typo3-cms/>`_
of the TYPO3 Slack Workspace can be used to get in direct contact with other
users!

If you are not registered yet, follow this
`guide <https://typo3.org/community/meet/chat-slack/>`_.
